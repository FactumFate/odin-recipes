Homemade Lasagna Sheets

Description

Homemade lasagna noodles are easier to make than you think and you don't even need a pasta machine. And forget about blanching the sheets before baking â€” no precooking is required!

Ingredients

  • 21/4 cups Italian-style tipo 00 flour, plus additional for dusting
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon water as needed

Steps

  1. make a vulcano of flour and crack inside the eggs;add salt
  2. mix it; add water too stiff
  3. beat it and spank it until nice ball
  4. place in bowl and cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate for 30min-1hour
  5. use a rolling pin and make it flaaat
  6. give the final form with a knife and let air-dry for 30min befor assembling a lasagna
